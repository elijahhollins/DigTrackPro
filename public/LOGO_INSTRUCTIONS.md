# Logo Setup

Place your company logo file in this directory as `logo.png`.

The logo is displayed in:
- The login page (left brand panel and mobile header)
- The top-left corner of the dashboard sidebar on all app pages

**Recommended specifications:**
- Format: PNG with transparent background (or SVG named `logo.png`)
- Aspect ratio: roughly square or horizontal works best
- Minimum size: 200×200 px
- The image will be rendered at 36×36 px (sidebar) and 44×44 px (login panel)

Once you drop `logo.png` into this folder and redeploy, the logo will appear automatically.
